# MAMMOTH

Mammoths are towering, elephant-like humanoids with broad frames and thick, leathery skin that gives them natural resilience. Their average height ranges from 7 to 8 feet, and their heads feature large ears and impressive tusks, which are often polished or decorated with carvings.

## ANCESTRY FEATURES

***Thick Hide:*** When you take Minor damage, you can **mark 2 Stress** instead of marking a Hit Point.

***Unwavering Constitution:*** **Spend 3 Hope** to halve incoming physical damage.
